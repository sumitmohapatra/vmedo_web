How it works

  1. Create your card

    Take the first step of saving your own life. Click the button below, enter relevant info, and create your Emergency Card. You will now be able to download your card.

  2. Carry your card

    We never know when & where an emergency can occur. So carry the card in your wallet. You can also get the emergency card printed and paste the QR code on your phone, helmets, and car windshields.

  3. During an emergency, any bystander or doctor will scan the card's QR code, and access the necessary info to administer the right first aid. They can also notify your emergency contacts using the same feature.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  1. Why should I create this emergency card?

    One of the reasons for delayed emergency care, after a medical emergency like road accidents, is said to be the information gap. A bystander who wants to help you after the accident is hesitant to provide first aid because he doesn't know your medical history, which is also needed by the doctor to initiate treatment.
    If you create this emergency card for yourself, with just a scan of the QR code on the card, a bystander can access your critical medical information, and also, call up your emergency contacts and inform about the accident.
    This Emergency Card is a simple solution to bridging the information gap during medical emergencies.


  2. How safe is my data that I provide here?

    First of all, VMEDO collects only the details that are necessary during a medical emergency. VMEDO adheres to all data privacy norms (Link to privacy policy) Also, when someone tries to access your information by scanning the QR code, his/her contact details are gathered are shared with you. So you always know who tried to access your information.

  3. I have created my Emergency Card. What next?

    For the first 10,000 Emergency Cards ever created, VMEDO will send the Emergency Card to your doorstep.
    The package that you receive will contain the original Emergency Card, which you can keep with you in your wallets, handbags, etc. The package will also contain stickers of the QR codes which you can paste on your phone, helmet and car windshields, so that it is easily visible for a bystander who is trying to help you.


  4. Can I create this card for my parents and friends?

    Absolutely. This Emergency Card has been conceptualized to keep you and your family safe. One user can create 5 emergency cards on his own. You can always encourage your extended families and friends to create a card for themselves and then their loved ones too.


  5. Can I add my insurance details to this emergency card?

    Yes. You can add your insurance policy number to this Emergency Card, which may fasten the admission process & treatment in a hospital or any other medical centre.


  6. Is this card linked to any Govt. Schemes?

    No. The Emergency Card is not linked to any Govt. or any other schemes. But one can include their ABHA no. (Ayushman Bharat Health Account no.) with this Emergency Card so that it is easier for a medical professional to access your previous medical history.
