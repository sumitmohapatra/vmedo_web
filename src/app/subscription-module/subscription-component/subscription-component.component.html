Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Is this an insurance subscription?

No, this is not insurance. This is an Emergency Medical Service (EMS) subscription only. This subscription ensures 24x7 Medical Emergency assistance service to your entire family across India and also gives access to value-added services like Free doctor consultation, Free Emergency ambulance and also discounts on other services booked on the platform.

How to add family member to a subscription?

Simple. Once you create your account on the platform, you can add/delete or Modify details of family members by visiting the profile section. Once the member is added, you can download the Emergency card of the respective member.

How to get Emergency assistance?

Simply call our 24x7 Helpline number and mention your Emergency card number to the executive to confirm your subscription. Based on your subscription our executives shall deliver the required services.

How to get a free doctor consultation?

Call our 24x7 Helpline number and mention your Emergency card number to the executive to confirm your subscription. If your subscription has the benefit of Free doctor consultation, the executive will immediately connect you to our in-house doctor for consultation.

How to book free Emergency Ambulance?

Call our 24x7 Helpline number and mention your Emergency card number to the executive to confirm your subscription. If your subscription covers free ambulance, our executives will arrange a Free ambulance to transfer the patient to the nearest Hospital. The benefit is applicable only to cardholders, and only in case of an emergency hospital admission (incident spot to hospital emergency bed). Free ambulance doesn't apply for regular hospital visits, discharge from the hospital, or inter-hospital transfers.

What if VMEDO can’t send a free ambulance?

If VMEDO cannot arrange a free ambulance (incident spot to a hospital emergency bed) for its premium subscriber due to the unavailability of VMEDO fleet nearby, upon confirmation from VMEDO, subscriber can book any private ambulance to take the patient to the nearest hospital. Ambulance expenses for that particular trip will be reimbursed by VMEDO to the subscriber upon submission of the bill.

How to get discounts at the hospital?

Discounts at the partner hospitals to VMEDO subscribers are applicable only for the admissions initiated from VMEDO. A discount coupon will be issued from VMEDO immediately after admission, and the benefit of a discount can be availed during billing.

How to get discounts on Non-Emergency ambulance bookings?

Call our 24x7 Helpline number and mention your emergency card number to the executive to confirm your subscription. Upon confirmation of your subscription, our executive will book an ambulance at a discounted price.

How to get discounts on medical items?

Log in to our e-commerce portal, and Enter coupon code shared during registration (via Email) at checkout to get the applicable discount.
