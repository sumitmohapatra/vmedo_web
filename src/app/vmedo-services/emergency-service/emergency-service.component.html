When to call?

Hospital Information

Not every hospital can handle every emergency, Choosing the right hospital during a Medical emergency plays a vital role in saving a life. We assist you with a list of verified Hospitals based on the medical emergency and help you with the right information on Emergency beds availability, ICU and Doctor availability to accept emergencies at the hospital at that moment. We also assist patients/guardians with hospital admission and support them during their tenure at the hospital for additional support including ambulance and blood.

Ambulance Booking

Finding the right ambulance, negotiating with them for a better price can be stressful during Medical Emergencies. We assist users to get the nearest verified ambulance at a fixed affordable price and support them throughout their journey to ensure, quick, safe and quality ambulance service without any hassles from the service provider.

Doctor Consultation

Not every Medical emergency needs hospital admission. Majority of the symptoms can be handled at home to avoid an unwanted hospital visit. Speak to our in-house Emergency Doctors to get immediate treatment advice or consultation to manage Medical Emergencies efficiently.
