VMEDO-Corporate

Minor injuries and illness

Our team of experienced healthcare professionals is available on-demand to provide timely diagnosis, treatment, and relief for common ailments such as colds, flu, sprains, cuts, and more. Skip the wait at the clinic and receive personalized care right where you are, ensuring convenience and peace of mind for you and your loved ones.

Wound dressing at home

Our skilled nurses will provide expert wound assessment, cleansing, and dressing changes tailored to your specific needs. Whether you're recovering from surgery, managing chronic wounds, or dealing with minor injuries, we'll ensure proper care and hygiene to support your healing journey without the hassle of traveling to a clinic

Injection (IV/IM) and drug delivery

Our licensed nurses will administer your prescribed injections safely and comfortably, saving you time and the hassle of traveling to a clinic. Whether you require routine vaccinations, ongoing medication injections, or specialty treatments, we provide personalized care in a familiar environment, ensuring your health needs are met with ease and convenience

IV infusion at home

Our skilled healthcare team delivers expert care, administering prescribed IV medications, fluids, or nutrients safely and efficiently. Say goodbye to long waits in clinic queues we bring comprehensive IV therapy directly to your doorstep, ensuring comfort, convenience, and optimal treatment outcomes

Catheterization at home

Our skilled healthcare team ensures safe and hygienic catheter insertion, maintenance, and removal, tailored to your individual needs. Whether for short-term relief or ongoing management, we provide compassionate care and support to enhance your comfort and quality of life without the need to visit a medical facility

Vaccination at home

Our licensed healthcare professionals bring vaccines directly to your doorstep, providing peace of mind and protection against preventable diseases. Whether for routine immunizations, travel vaccinations, or flu shots, we offer personalized care in the comfort of your own home, saving you time and hassle while prioritizing your health

Suture removal at home

Our skilled healthcare team ensures gentle and precise removal of sutures, promoting optimal healing and comfort. Say goodbye to the inconvenience of scheduling appointments and traveling to clinics we bring expert care right to your doorstep, providing peace of mind and convenience during your recovery process

Ryles tube insertion at home

Our experienced healthcare professionals ensure safe and gentle placement of the Ryles tube, as well as ongoing monitoring and support tailored to your individual needs. Skip the inconvenience of hospital visits and receive expert care right where you are, ensuring comfort and convenience throughout your treatment journey.
